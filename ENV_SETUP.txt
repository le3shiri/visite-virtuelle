# Environment Variable Setup for Facebook Pixel

Add this to your .env.local file:

NEXT_PUBLIC_FACEBOOK_PIXEL_ID=1848322352445114


This is your actual Facebook Pixel ID from Meta Business Manager.

To find your Pixel ID:
1. Go to Facebook Events Manager (business.facebook.com/events_manager)
2. Select your pixel
3. Copy the Pixel ID from the top of the page

Note: The .env.local file is gitignored and won't be committed to your repository.
